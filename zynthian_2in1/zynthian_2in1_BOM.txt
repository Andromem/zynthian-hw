
Zynthian 2in1 (MIDI-IN + GPIO-expander)
---------------------------------------
1 x diodode 1N4148 (D1)
1 x opto-coupler 6N138
1 x resistor 1K (R1)
1 x resistor 10K (R2)
1 x resistor 220 (R3)
1 x I2C GPIO expander MCP23008
2 x ceramic capacitor 100nF (C1, C2)
1 x 8 pin-row angled male connector (GPIO-ext)
1 x 6 pin-row male connector (RBPi)
1 x 2 pin-row male connector (MIDI-IN)
